About Me

I'm an enthusiast beginner in a Tester's World!

The key function of a QA is to conduct manual or automated tests on software products. My ongoing goal as a software tester is to reduce the number of bugs in software and identify as many mistakes as possible. QAs are serving as a bridge between users and development team.

Skilled QAs exhibit an inexhaustible curiosity that drives their work, attention to details, analytical mindset and good team companionship. I believe that every QA should spend some time learning more about how and why things work, especially things that have to do with computers and software.

Starting with the basics I am working hard to reshape my career, step by step, with progressive growth, always curious, and seeking to continuously improve in the processes. Curiosity, good communication, and time management are skills that define me.
